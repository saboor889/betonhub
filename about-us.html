Our Story

We’re driven by our values

Bet On Hub started from a simple question, how can we make every day manual labor, chores, and tedious tasking a thing of the past for business-owners and consumers alike? A recent survey concluded that 94% of Canadians struggle with finding the right service provider in their area. Bet On Hub solves that problem by grouping local service providers and letting them compete for the job while maintaining anonymity for the client.

Rather than connecting with different providers one at a time, Bet On Hub will give users the peace of mind to connect with all of the respective service providers in their demographic’s radius without disclosing their information with just one click through the app. All they must do is:

  • • Choose the category of the service
  • • Describe the job that needs to be done
  • • Attach a picture or file (if needed for reference)
  • • Adjust the radius of their demographic
  • • Mention your schedule (a time slot)
  • • Choose the right price (your budget) to get the job done
  • • Book An Appointment!

The selected provider will connect with you via the Bet On Hub app to discuss details via our built in chat feature, lock in the pricing, and get the job done! The client’s information remains hidden until you “Book the Appointment”. Say goodbye to time consuming chores and hello to more free time. The world of services on your fingertips!

You may be thinking, what about the safety of allowing service providers in your home? Bet On Hub has your safety as it’s TOP PRIORITY. We ensure all providers are certified, licensed, or verified professionals prior to using the app. Our 24/7 support staff is standing by to help with any issues and make the process as effortless as possible, just Bet On Hub!
