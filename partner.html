Working App

How Does Bet On Hub App Work?

Bet On Hub’s powerful web design and integrated systems allows users to connect directly with service providers around their demographic. Not only do Service Providers get a description of the task that needs to be completed but they can also chat with the customers directly through the app to get more details before giving a quote or committing to anything. The pricing of the service will be discussed and locked before the task is performed to avoid any misunderstandings between the service provider and the customer. Let’s make life easy with just a click!

  • 1 Register your business on the App
  • 2 Wait for our team to approve
  • 3 Get service requests from customers
  • 4 Chat with customers in real time
  • 5 Book the service
  • 6 Complete the task!
